About us

FODMAPP was created to make the low-FODMAP diet easier and faster to follow in everyday life 🌿 – whether you're completely new to the diet or have been following it for a while.

Our story

The founder of FODMAPP developed IBS as an adult and knows how challenging it can be to start the FODMAP diet. Often you're just told to try things out, and many are left feeling alone in figuring out what works.

To make this easier, the app was developed – with a focus on speed, usability, and everyday confidence. First for personal use, but now available to everyone 🌎.

Our vision

We want to provide safety and support in your food choices – quickly and easily 🍽️. With features like AI chat, camera analysis, and barcode scanning, you won't have to spend unnecessary time and energy wondering what you can eat.

Our goal is that everyone who is told to start the FODMAP diet can do it as easily as we can today 💚. That's why the app is available in many languages 🌍, so users around the world can get help.
