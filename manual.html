FODMAPP Manual

FODMAPP gives you three powerful tools to navigate the low-FODMAP diet: the AI camera for photo analysis, the barcode scanner, and the AI chat assistant. Here's how to use each one.

📸 AI Camera

Everything you can photograph

Point the camera, take a photo – and get an instant FODMAP analysis with a score from 0 to 100.

🥕 Single food item

Photograph a fruit, vegetable, or ingredient to instantly find out if it's low or high FODMAP – with portion advice and safe alternatives.

🍲 Meals and dishes

Photo an entire meal and the AI identifies all ingredients, assesses each one, and checks for FODMAP stacking – where several moderate items together exceed the safe limit.

📋 Restaurant menus

Photo a menu in any language and get suggestions for safe dishes, with specific tips on what to ask the waiter to change or leave out.

🧊 Fridge contents

Photo your fridge and the AI lists all recognisable ingredients with FODMAP status, then creates two low-FODMAP recipe suggestions based on what you have.

🥤 Drinks

Photograph a drink – juice, smoothie, or soft drink – and get a full FODMAP assessment including information about sweeteners and safe portion sizes.

🔢 FODMAP score 0–100

Every camera analysis gives you a FODMAP score from 0 to 100. Green (80–100) means safe, yellow (55–79) means moderate caution, and red (below 55) means high FODMAP content – so you can decide at a glance.

🏷️ Barcode Scanner

Instant product checks in the store

Point the camera at any product's barcode and get a full FODMAP assessment in seconds.

🌍 Over 4.4 million products

The barcode scanner taps into Open Food Facts – one of the world's largest open food databases with over 4.4 million products from countries around the globe. Whether you're at home or travelling abroad, chances are the product is already in the database.

📊 AI-powered ingredient analysis

When you scan a barcode, the app fetches the full ingredient list and analyses every single ingredient with advanced AI – giving you a complete, reliable FODMAP verdict.

✅ Safe shopping made easy

Use the scanner before putting anything in your trolley. It works with packaged products from any country – perfect when travelling or shopping at international stores.

💬 AI Chat

What can you ask the AI?

Type a question and get an answer in seconds – in your own language, 24/7.

🥦 Evaluate any food

Type a food, dish, or product name and get an instant FODMAP rating with explanation, portion advice, and safe alternatives.

🍽️ Analyse restaurant dishes

Describe a restaurant dish and the AI assesses each ingredient, explains the FODMAP risks, and suggests how to order it low-FODMAP.

📅 Weekly meal plans

Ask for a full low-FODMAP weekly menu with breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks – the AI creates a varied and balanced plan with recipes.

🛒 Shopping lists

After your meal plan, ask for a shopping list. The AI generates one based on the menu so you always have the right ingredients at home.

❓ FODMAP questions

Ask what FODMAP is, which types exist (fructans, galactans, polyols, fructose, lactose), how the diet works, or anything else related to FODMAP.

🧘 Stress management

Stress can worsen IBS symptoms. Ask the AI for relaxation techniques, breathing exercises, and practical tips for long-term stress management.

✦ Tips

Good to know

📸 Take clear photos

Make sure there's good lighting and the entire food item or menu is visible in the camera frame for the best analysis.

📶 Internet connection

The camera and barcode features require an internet connection. WiFi gives the fastest and most reliable response.

⭐ Build routines

Use the app whenever you're in doubt – at home, at the supermarket, or at a restaurant. The more you use it, the more confident you'll become.

🔒 Privacy first

Images are automatically deleted within 24 hours after analysis. We never store your chat conversations.

📏 Portion size matters

FODMAP content depends on how much you eat. Try to include the portion size when chatting with the AI – it makes the answer more accurate.

🗓️ Use the app throughout the process

The FODMAP diet has three phases: elimination, reintroduction, and personalisation. FODMAPP supports you through all of them.
