✦ Explore features

Three tools. One app.

Select a feature to see what it can do for you.

Ask a question and get an answer in seconds – tailored to you.

🥦 Evaluate any food

Type a food, dish, or product name and get an instant FODMAP rating with explanation, portion advice, and safe alternatives.

🍽️ Analyse restaurant dishes

Describe a restaurant dish and the AI assesses each ingredient, explains the FODMAP risks, and suggests how to order it low-FODMAP.

📅 Weekly meal plans

Ask for a full low-FODMAP weekly menu with breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks – the AI creates a varied and balanced plan with recipes.

🛒 Shopping lists

After your meal plan, ask for a shopping list. The AI generates one based on the menu so you always have the right ingredients at home.

❓ FODMAP questions

Ask what FODMAP is, which types exist (fructans, galactans, polyols, fructose, lactose), how the diet works, or anything else related to FODMAP.

🧘 Stress management

Stress can worsen IBS symptoms. Ask the AI for relaxation techniques, breathing exercises, and practical tips for long-term stress management.

Point, scan and get answers – straight from your camera.

🥕 Single food item

Photograph a fruit, vegetable, or ingredient to instantly find out if it's low or high FODMAP – with portion advice and safe alternatives.

🍲 Meals and dishes

Photo an entire meal and the AI identifies all ingredients, assesses each one, and checks for FODMAP stacking – where several moderate items together exceed the safe limit.

📋 Restaurant menus

Photo a menu in any language and get suggestions for safe dishes, with specific tips on what to ask the waiter to change or leave out.

🧊 Fridge contents

Photo your fridge and the AI lists all recognisable ingredients with FODMAP status, then creates two low-FODMAP recipe suggestions based on what you have.

🥤 Drinks

Photograph a drink – juice, smoothie, or soft drink – and get a full FODMAP assessment including information about sweeteners and safe portion sizes.

🔢 FODMAP score 0–100

Every camera analysis gives you a FODMAP score from 0 to 100. Green (80–100) means safe, yellow (55–79) means moderate caution, and red (below 55) means high FODMAP content – so you can decide at a glance.

📊 AI-powered ingredient analysis

When you scan a barcode, the app fetches the full ingredient list and analyses every single ingredient with advanced AI – giving you a complete, reliable FODMAP verdict.

✦ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the low-FODMAP diet?

▶

A research-based dietary approach to reduce digestive issues in people with IBS, by limiting certain hard-to-digest carbohydrates.

How do you handle privacy?

▶

Images are stored temporarily on a secure Firebase server for analysis and automatically deleted within 24 hours. Text in the AI chat is not stored by us. No personal data is shared with third parties for marketing.

Is the app available on iOS and Android?

▶

Yes, FODMAPP is available for both iPhone and Android phones.

How accurate is the AI?

▶

FODMAPP uses advanced AI that provides reliable FODMAP guidance based on established research. The AI should be used alongside professional medical advice – not as a replacement.

Is the app free?

▶

FODMAPP offers a free tier with 20 AI interactions per month. For more, upgrade to FODMAPP Plus (300/month) or FODMAPP Pro (1000/month). Subscriptions are available as monthly or yearly plans.

What languages does the app support?

▶

FODMAPP is available in over 23 languages including English, Norwegian, Swedish, Danish, Finnish, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Turkish, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Hindi, Indonesian, Lithuanian, Thai, and Arabic.
