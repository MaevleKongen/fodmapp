✦ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the low-FODMAP diet?

▶

A research-based dietary approach to reduce digestive issues in people with IBS, by limiting certain hard-to-digest carbohydrates.

How do you handle privacy?

▶

Images are stored temporarily on a secure server for analysis and deleted immediately after. No personal data is stored permanently.

Is the app available on iOS and Android?

▶

Yes, FODMAPP is available for both iPhone and Android phones.

How accurate is the AI?

▶

The AI is trained on established FODMAP research and provides reliable guidance. It should be used alongside professional medical advice – not as a replacement.

Is the app free?

▶

FODMAPP offers a free version with basic features. For unlimited AI use and full access, upgrade to FODMAPP Plus.

What languages does the app support?

▶

FODMAPP is available in over 23 languages including English, Norwegian, Swedish, Danish, Finnish, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Turkish, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Hindi, Indonesian, Lithuanian, Thai, and Arabic.
