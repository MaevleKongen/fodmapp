Deleting your account and personal data

Here you can find information about how to request deletion of your account, which data we store, and how data is processed in FODMAPP.

Which data we store

When you create an account in FODMAPP, we store: email address, first name, last name, password, which subscription type you have (Free or Plus), and how many AI prompts you have used in the current month.

When you submit images to the AI feature, the images are stored temporarily on our servers for up to 24 hours before they are automatically deleted. The text you write in the AI chat is never stored by us.

Text and images you send to the AI are processed by Google Gemini in accordance with Google’s own terms and privacy policy.

Sign in with Google

If you choose to sign in with Google (OAuth), we receive the name and email address associated with your Google account. This is only used to create and log into your FODMAPP account.

How to request deletion of your account

Send us an email at post@fodmapp.com with:
– that you want to delete your account
– the email address your account is registered with

We will confirm your request and delete your account within a reasonable time.

If you have used Google sign-in, you can also disconnect FODMAPP access via your Google account settings.

What is deleted?

When your account is deleted, the following is removed: name, email address, subscription type, AI usage, and any images that are still stored temporarily. Text and images processed by Google Gemini may remain stored by Google according to their rules — this is outside our control.
